Development

The Merchants Guild is still in development and not ready for use. However we are progressing fast and want to have something working as soon as possible. If you're interested, join our mailing list above and we will keep you up to date with important updates. (I promise not to spam you)

About

The Merchants Guild is being developed in Sydney, Nova Scotia. Home to sweet tech companies and avid LARPers.

I'm the lead developer, Ben MacNeil. I'm both a producer and consumer of LARP goods and have experienced lots of problems with trying to buy and sell gear. This lead me to start work on The Merchants Guild.
